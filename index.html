About Us

HAMMOND ENGINEERING is a machine shop based in Bairnsdale, Victoria. Our services include CAD, CNC milling (4 axis) and turning (6 axis) and manual turning, for prototypes and one offs, through to large production runs.



CNC Engineering

With our 3 CNC machines we are able to offer a great range of capabilities. From bar fed bulk production Lathe work, to intricate 4 axis milling. We are also able to offer MIG, TIG (Stainless steel, aluminium and mild steel) and arc welding when required. Give us a call or send a facebook message to get your job quoted.
